Glycemic Index Values for Common Foods

Low GI (55 or less) - Best for blood sugar control:
- Steel-cut oats: GI 42, 27g carbs per cup
- Quinoa: GI 53, 39g carbs per cup
- Sweet potato: GI 54, 27g carbs per medium
- Apple: GI 38, 25g carbs per medium
- Lentils: GI 32, 40g carbs per cup
- Chickpeas: GI 28, 45g carbs per cup
- Greek yogurt (unsweetened): GI 11, 9g carbs per cup
- Berries (strawberries, blueberries): GI 25-40, 15g carbs per cup
- Nuts (almonds, walnuts): GI 15, 6g carbs per ounce

Medium GI (56-69) - Moderate impact:
- Brown rice: GI 68, 45g carbs per cup
- Whole wheat bread: GI 69, 12g carbs per slice
- Banana (ripe): GI 62, 27g carbs per medium
- Honey: GI 61, 17g carbs per tablespoon
- Regular oatmeal: GI 58, 27g carbs per cup

High GI (70+) - Causes rapid spikes:
- White bread: GI 75, 14g carbs per slice
- White rice: GI 73, 45g carbs per cup
- Cornflakes: GI 81, 26g carbs per cup
- Baked potato: GI 85, 37g carbs per medium
- Watermelon: GI 76, 11g carbs per cup
- Rice cakes: GI 82, 7g carbs per cake
- Pretzels: GI 83, 23g carbs per ounce
- White pasta: GI 71, 43g carbs per cup

Tips for lowering GI:
- Add protein or healthy fats to meals
- Choose whole grains over refined
- Eat vegetables first
- Don't overcook pasta (al dente has lower GI)
- Combine high-GI foods with low-GI foods
